    History:

    Copenhagen Dating back to near the 10th Century, starting off as a small village, Copenhagen has been thriving on Denmarks coastal Islands Zealand and Amager. Since the 15th Century, Copenhagen has been the nation's capital city and home to the Royal Family. Denmark has always been known as one of the happiest countries in the world, with the worlds 2nd oldest amusement park (the inspiration for Disney World) you'd understand why! The colourful houses and beautiful canals have made Denmark and Copenhagen constant icons throughout the world!
